When was Program Implementation Unit (PIU) established?

PIU was established in 2017

What is program implementation unit (piu) ?

Program Implementation Unit has been established by Planning & Development Department, Government of the Punjab to coordinate and monitor program activities of important donor funded programs including Jobs & Competitiveness (J&C) Program for Results and Strengthening Markets for Agriculture and Rural Transformation in Punjab (SMART Punjab). The PIU is responsible for overall monitoring and coordination of J&C and SMART programs. The unit also performs the functions of providing technical support to the implementing agencies as and when required through engagement of technical assistance.
